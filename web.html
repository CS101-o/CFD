THE PROBLEM

Manual Parameter Tweaking

Engineers spend weeks manually tweaking design parameters in simulation tools and CFD software, running endless trial-and-error cycles to optimize performance.

Ideal for Automation

Much of this iterative work does not require deep technical expertise and is ideally suited for automation. Our informal surveys show that engineering students complain about the tweaking process.

OUR SOLUTION

No-Code Design Optimization Platform

We are developing a no-code design optimization platform to empower engineers to automatically improve design efficiency without needing programming knowledge. However, each field or even business would require different models.

Our big plan is to develop the technology and turn research along with help of supervisors from the University of Toronto into business innovation and then find customers.

TECHNOLOGY

Following an academic review of comparable methods, utilizing NIFs/PINOs to create CFD designs is a relatively new field of study that holds immense potential.

Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINO)

Creates functional mappings between input airfoils and output pressure fields while respecting physical laws.

Neural Implicit Flow (NIF)

Generates mesh-agnostic approximations for lift, drag, and moment coefficients.

Automated Dataset Generation

Combines airfoil databases with XFoil and Ansys Fluent for comprehensive training data.
